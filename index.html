Our Services

We provide end-to-end field services for network operators, civil contractors, developers, and local councils, including:

FTTP / FTTC / FTTN Network Construction

We provide comprehensive network construction services for Fibre to the Premises (FTTP), Fibre to the Curb (FTTC), and Fibre to the Node (FTTN) technologies. This includes greenfield and brownfield rollout, pit and conduit infrastructure, lead-in conduit (LIC) provisioning, lead-in cable installation, and connection to active equipment. All works comply with NBN Co and local council standards.

Pit and Pipe Installation

Our team delivers full-service underground infrastructure installation, including trench excavation, pit installation (Class B & Class D), conduit laying (20mm–100mm), trench backfill, compaction, and surface restoration. All works are dial-before-you-dig (DBYD) compliant, and follow AS/NZS 2053 and NBN pit and pipe installation guidelines.

Directional Drilling (HDD)

We provide Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) solutions for installing underground conduits beneath roads, driveways, railways, and environmentally sensitive areas. Our service includes utility locating, bore path design, pilot hole drilling, reaming, and conduit pulling. HDD is performed using Vermeer and Ditch Witch rigs, operated by licensed and VOC-certified professionals.

Fibre Hauling and Cable Installation

Our fibre hauling services cover internal building pathways and external underground routes, using manual and mechanical methods. We handle hauling of single-mode, multi-mode, and ribbon fibre cables through complex routes, including pits, manholes, and risers. All installations follow bend radius control and maximum pulling tension standards.

Fibre Splicing and Testing (OTDR & LTS)

We offer precision fusion splicing, connectorisation, and full testing services using industry-leading equipment (Fujikura, Sumitomo, EXFO). Testing includes Optical Time Domain Reflectometer (OTDR), Light Source & Power Meter (LTS), and Insertion Loss testing, with results delivered in Level A/B format per NBN and Telstra specs.

Civil and Restoration Works

Our civil works team executes trenching, excavation, concrete cutting, asphalt reinstatement, and landscaping restoration. All works follow traffic management plans, environmental control procedures (e.g. sediment barriers), and site-specific safety plans (SWMS, JSA). We also coordinate permits and notifications with local councils and authorities.

Project Management and Consulting

We provide end-to-end project management solutions including site scoping, job pack preparation, resource scheduling, progress tracking, and compliance reporting. Our consultants support fibre rollout planning, network upgrade strategies, QA/QC documentation, and liaison with NBN, Opticomm, Superloop and other major network providers.

Network Maintenance and Emergency Fault Repair

Our maintenance teams are on call 24/7 for proactive and reactive fault response. We conduct network diagnostics, joint enclosure repairs, fibre break restoration, signal loss rectification, and emergency rerouting. Services are available for both underground and aerial assets, with full traceability and reporting via OTDR and fault locators.

Subcontracting and Turnkey Delivery

We operate as a trusted subcontractor for Tier 1 and Tier 2 NBN delivery partners, offering labour, plant, materials, and full work package execution. From design verification and build to testing and as-built submission, our turnkey delivery model ensures quality, compliance, and on-time handover across diverse projects.
