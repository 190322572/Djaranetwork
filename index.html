DJARANETWORK SERVICE Pty Ltd is a professional telecommunications infrastructure service provider based in Australia.

We specialise in comprehensive network construction services including fibre optic installation, pit and pipe construction, horizontal directional drilling (HDD), and fibre splicing and testing.

Our mission is to connect communities with faster, more reliable networks — and to do so with integrity, safety, and efficiency.

Our Services

We provide end-to-end field services for network operators, civil contractors, developers, and local councils, including:

FTTP/FTTC/FTTN Network Construction

We specialise in the design, installation, and commissioning of Fibre to the Premises (FTTP), Fibre to the Curb (FTTC), and Fibre to the Node (FTTN) solutions across urban and regional areas. Our skilled technicians ensure high-speed, low-loss fibre infrastructure for residential, commercial, and government clients.

Pits and Conduit Installation

We deliver end-to-end underground infrastructure construction including trenching, directional boring, pit installation, and conduit laying. All works comply with NBN and Telco industry standards, ensuring safety, alignment, and future-proof network integration.

Fibre Hauling and Cable Installation

Our certified crews carry out fibre hauling using best-practice techniques for both existing and new conduit systems. We handle all types of cable including single-mode, multi-mode, and hybrid bundles, with minimal disruption to public infrastructure.

Fibre Splicing and Testing (OTDR & LTS)

We provide high-precision fibre splicing, joint enclosures, and patching, followed by OTDR (Optical Time-Domain Reflectometer) and LTS (Light Source Testing) for quality assurance. Our reports meet NBN handover and compliance requirements.

Civil and Restoration Works

From concrete reinstatement to landscaping, our team ensures all civil works are completed to the highest standards. We understand the importance of community and council compliance and ensure minimal impact post-installation.

Project Management and Consulting

With decades of combined experience, our project managers coordinate large-scale NBN rollouts and private sector telecom projects from planning to execution. We also provide consultancy for compliance, permits, and design approvals.

Maintenance and Fault Response Services

Our rapid-response teams are available for scheduled maintenance and emergency fault repairs. We operate 24/7 services to minimise network downtime and ensure uninterrupted service to end users.

Why Choose Us

Over a Decade of NBN Project Experience

Our team includes seasoned professionals with over a decade of hands-on experience across all NBN network types including FTTP, FTTC, HFC, FTTN, and FTTB. We ensure every deployment is compliant with NBN Co’s technical and construction standards.

Full-Scope Capability — One-Stop Service

We deliver end-to-end project execution — from trenching, pit and conduit installation, fibre pulling, splicing to OTDR testing and handover — ensuring faster delivery, lower costs and fully controlled quality without third-party dependency.

Fully Licensed & Compliant

Our crew is fully certified under Australian standards, holding White Cards, NBN construction permits, Telstra accreditations, confined space certifications, traffic control licenses, and asbestos awareness training — ensuring full regulatory compliance.

Rapid Response & Smart Scheduling

With dedicated dispatchers and an integrated logistics system, we can mobilise technical teams and equipment within 24 hours across Australia — ideal for NBN emergency call-outs and urgent installations.

Fully Equipped with In-House Machinery

We own and operate our own HDD rigs, vacuum excavators, chain trenchers, fusion splicing units, and OTDR testers — reducing reliance on third parties and ensuring consistent high-quality performance.
